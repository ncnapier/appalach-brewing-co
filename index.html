About Us

Appalach Brewing Company is proud to be the first and only craft brewery on the Cumberland Plateau. Appalach was started when childhood friends Zach and Nathaniel decided to use their expertise (of food-service management and craft brewing) to bring great beer to the heart of the Cumberland. Inspired by the wonderful and unique beauty of the Cumberland Plateau, we craft all of our beers to capture the natural wonder of our home. Whether you're a local, on vacation, or just passing through- we welcome you into our taproom to experience the flavor of the Appalachians in every drink.

Our Team

Nathaniel Napier: A biochemist by training- Nathaniel harnesses his background in laboratory sciences to precisely craft beers that reflect the wild wonder of the Cumberland.
